About Me

I'm a Management Information Systems student at MSOE. In May 2017, I'll graduate with a degree in Management Information Systems. I will also graducate with a degree from the Fachhochschule Lübeck in Lübeck, Germany, where I studied in my 3rd year.

Technology has always been a passion of mine and I like to dabble in many different things. I've worked for the MSOE Digital Marketing department as a camera assistant where I shot and edited a large amount of video. Now during my senior year, I work for the MSOE Humanities, Social Sciences, and Communicaitons department managing their social media. I shoot and edit video and photo content to post on the social media channels I created for the department.

I have experience administrating Linux, mac OS, and Windows server and desktop systems. I am also familiar with Java, JavaScript, Python, C/C++, and SQL (and SQL-like database systems). I'm passionate about technology and do my best to keep up with all facets of the industry as much as possible, epsecailly concerning security. For information on what this site uses, check out the GitHub page.

I'm a member of the MSOE chapter of Phi Beta Lamda (PBL), the world's largest student-run professional organization. In my sophomore year, I was elected Treasurer and kept track of organization finances and authorized purchases. That year, we went to Chicago for the PBL National Conference, where I placed 4th in the Computer Concepts competition. I plan on competing in the spring and regional conferences in this upcoming year.
