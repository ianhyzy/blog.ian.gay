Camp for Kids Redesign

I redesigned Milwaukee's Camps for Kids website to make it look great on all devices and made it easy for staff to make changes.

View Site More info

About the Redesign

I started the redesign with a partner in October 2015. We used Wordpress becuase the client wanted to be able to easily update and edit the site in the future. They gave us the copy and images, and a list of few things they wanted (such as the footer with logo and hit counter). I found an excellent theme to start with and had the first prototype done in a few days. The new site works great on screens of all sizes and shapes and it's easier than ever for parents to find camps that are right for their kids.
