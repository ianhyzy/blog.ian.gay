Welcome to our home

We are located in the heart of Milwaukee, a city with a rich cultural history. Milwaukee hosts dozens of festivals, including Summerfest, the world's largest music festival. The home of Harley-Davidson lives here. At one time, the world's four largest breweries were based here (Schlitz, Pabst, Blatz, and Miller) which has earned the city it's endearing nickname, the "Brew City".

Make a pilgrimage to the "holy city" of beer

Milwaukee still has a burning passion for it's beer culture. It is truly a beer-lover's paradise, with dozens of local breweries, including our very own in-house brewpub, you'll find no better destination for craft beer fanatics, sports fans, college students on break, or somebody who just likes to try something different.
