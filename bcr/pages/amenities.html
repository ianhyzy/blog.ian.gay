Everything you desire

The Brew City resort has something to keep everyone relaxed and entertained.
view_daysee what we offer
Take a tour of our in house-brewery

We've designed our own custom microbrew, the Bronze Bear, and you can watch a bottle made from scratch right in our brewery. At the end of the tour, step behind the bar and pour yourself a tall glass - you've earned it.

Better than any sports bar

Watch the Packer game and play a round of billiards, or just sit in our Italian leather easy chairs with a great drink. If you're in the mood for a fine cigar, take a walk on into our humidor and enjoy one of the world's best cigars in The Grey Lounge.

Taste in the best cheese in country

Want to experience some local specialities? Grab a cold Brew City beer and try more cheese than could have ever imagined. Wisconsin is world-famous for the quality and variety of cheeses - and you can try them all here.

Try the winner of the U.S. Championship Cheese contest, Sartori's Reserve Black Pepper BellaVitano.

Press away the stress with a massage
  • Swedish
  • Hot Rock
  • Shiatsu

The classic massage method. Great for first-timers or those who wish for a calming, full-body massage. This classic massage will release long-term muscle tension in your back and shoulders.

Great for cold winter days, a hot rock massage can penetrate the skin deeper than a traditional message and with less force than a Swedish massage. Hot Rock Massages can promote blood flow, reduce stress, and promote deep relaxation.

Shiatsu massages are an ancient Japanese art that differs from other message types in that it uses more focused pressure, with fingers and thumbs, to message. It can therefore be done clothed.
