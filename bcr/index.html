Events

Brew City Resort works with hundreds of local business and organizations to give BCR guests one-of-a-kind access to the hottest events in Milwaukee.

Dining

In addition to our in-house craft brew, we have over 200 craft beers available for your drinking pleasure. Pair with excellent locally-inspired eats served up by our four-star chefs.

Amenities

Whether you want to relax in our spa with a massage, catch the game with a fresh brew in our lounge, or dance the night away with the city's best DJs, we can meet your every desire.

Experience Our In-House Brewery

To provide our guests with the quintessential brew experience of Milwaukee, we've built a brewer on-site, and you're welcome to take a tour and try a glass of our homemade Brown Bear lager anytime. We're proud to contribute to the local craft brew scene and feature over eighty different craft brews in our pub. Our experienced bartenders can help you select a flight of brews to enjoy while snacking on our beer-battered cheese curds or a real German-style bratwurst. Our gastronomy is second to none in authenticity and quality. Don't miss the traditional Friday Fish fry with the freshest cod and best fries.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Brew City resort is the premiere resort for those who want to experience the best of Milwaukee under one roof. We proudly host the Midwest's best beverages, foods, and entertainment experiences.
